About me

I am a hairdresser with many years of experience. Working with hair is not just my profession but also my joy. I have valuable international experience from hair salons in Australia, which have enriched and inspired me. I strive to bring that unforgettable energy and creativity into my work here in Prague. My primary goal is to preserve the health of my clients' hair, which is why I prefer gentle, natural-based products. You will find a wide range of services with me, including the amazing Agave Smoothing Treatment, which can effectively regenerate and smooth tired or damaged hair for up to four months. If you dream of longer or thicker hair, I offer you the gentlest method of hair extensions using the Tape In method, which utilizes hair tapes. Come visit me at La Noire salon. I look forward to seeing you and helping you achieve the beautiful hair you deserve!" Vlaďka
